# FoodCourt Support

## Getting Help

Thanks for using FoodCourt! If you need help or have questions, here's how to reach us.

## Common Questions

### How do I rate a dish?
1. Find the restaurant (via Nearby or Search)
2. Tap the dish you want to rate
3. Tap the **+** button or "Add Verdict"
4. Select your rating and add optional notes

### How do I add a dish that's not listed?
1. Go to the restaurant page
2. Tap **"Add Dish"**
3. Enter the dish name and details
4. New dishes may require approval before appearing

### How do I find friends?
1. Go to your **Profile** tab
2. Tap **"Add Friends"**
3. Search by username or find contacts already on FoodCourt

### How do I change my verdict visibility?
When rating a dish, you can choose:
- **Public** - Everyone can see
- **Friends** - Only your followers can see
- **Private** - Only you can see

### How do I delete my account?
Email us at [foodcourt.app.help@gmail.com](mailto:foodcourt.app.help@gmail.com) with your username, and we'll process your deletion request within 30 days.

## Report a Bug

Found something broken? Please let us know by emailing [foodcourt.app.help@gmail.com](mailto:foodcourt.app.help@gmail.com) with:
- What happened
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Your device and iOS version
