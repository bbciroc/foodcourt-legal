# FoodCourt Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 3, 2025**

## Overview

FoodCourt ("we", "our", or "the app") is a dish-rating app that helps you discover great food through friends' recommendations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Apple ID**: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address
- **Username & Display Name**: The profile name you create in the app
- **Profile Photo**: Optional avatar you may upload

### User-Generated Content
- **Verdicts**: Your dish ratings, notes, and photos
- **Sessions**: Groups of ratings from a single restaurant visit
- **Follows**: The users you choose to follow

### Location Data
- **Device Location**: When you grant permission, we use your location to show nearby restaurants
- Location is only accessed when the app is in use and is not stored on our servers

### Contacts (Optional)
- If you choose to find friends, we temporarily hash phone numbers from your contacts to match with other users
- Raw contact data is never stored or transmitted

### Automatically Collected
- Basic app usage analytics
- Crash reports to improve stability

## How We Use Your Information

- Display your profile and verdicts to other users (based on your visibility settings)
- Show you verdicts from users you follow
- Find restaurants and dishes near you
- Improve the app experience
- Respond to support requests

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on [Supabase](https://supabase.com), a cloud database provider with servers in the United States. Data is protected by:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Row-level security policies
- Secure authentication via Apple Sign-In

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for targeted advertising

We may share data only:
- With service providers necessary to operate the app (Supabase, Apple)
- If required by law or legal process

## Your Privacy Controls

### Verdict Visibility
You control who sees each verdict:
- **Public**: Visible to all users
- **Friends**: Visible only to users who follow you
- **Private**: Visible only to you

### Account Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app: **Profile → Settings (gear icon) → Data & Privacy → Delete Account**.

## Children's Privacy

FoodCourt is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by other means.
